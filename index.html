The Pride of MAHARASHTRA

RooBaroo is the annual techno-management and cultural festival of NMITD, organized by the student community with the support of the Faculties, Principal and Management. Several intercollegiate events are organised as part of RooBaroo which also features an annual social gathering and convocation ceremony. The theme chosen for this year's Roobaroo is "The Pride of Maharashtra" which focuses on the uniqueness and achievements of the state Maharashtra. The main motive of chosing this theme is to showcase the contributions of Maharashtra for our country as well as the whole world. Many artworks, sketches, paintings, and other decorative elements that highlighted the concept are placed throughout the entire campus which indeed creates a feeling of pride to have Maharashtra as our state, our nation's state.
